Selected Work

Projects that represent my current trajectory.

Security Research · Team Lead May 2025 – Jul. 2025

FL Breaker: Security Weakness of Federated Learning

Reproduced DLG-style privacy attacks on federated learning, evaluated reconstruction quality, analyzed the optimization bottlenecks of gradient inversion, and compared defense paths including homomorphic encryption, secure multi-party computation, and differential privacy.

  • Worked in a 5-member NUS workshop team on privacy vulnerabilities in federated learning.
  • Built live loss-curve visualizations to show private image reconstruction from random initialization.
  • Co-authored the final survey deliverable that received Third Prize.
Integrated Circuits · Lab Member Oct. 2024 – Dec. 2024

Full-Custom SN74HC259 in 0.18 μm CMOS

Participated in the complete circuit-to-layout flow of an 8-bit addressable latch, including schematic design, pre-layout simulation, layout implementation, post-layout extraction, and timing/power verification in Cadence Virtuoso and Spectre.

  • Contributed to D-latch, inverter, NAND/NOR, buffer, and I/O cell schematic design.
  • Completed partial custom layout under area and design-rule constraints.
  • Verified DRC/LVS cleanliness and confirmed design targets in post-layout simulation.
